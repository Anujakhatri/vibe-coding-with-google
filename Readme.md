## 💡 What is "Vibe Coding"?

Vibe coding is the iterative, highly-interactive process of working alongside AI assistants and agents to build software. Instead of writing every line manually, we focus on orchestrating AI, providing the right context, architecture, and intent (the "vibe"), and letting the AI do the heavy lifting.
